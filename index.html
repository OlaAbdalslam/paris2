Classic Room:

Our Classic Room's Parisian style surrounds you with
characteristic warmth and luxury. It's perfect for business travellers
who want to sleep deeply and feel cossetted in sheer splendour.

Superior Room:

Classical Parisian style is reinvented for the modern era in this
beautifully bright, airy room. A wonderful sense of peace and quiet awaits.

Deluxe Room:

This spacious room is full of natural light and original artworks
carefully chosen by our designers. Like many other rooms, it can
connect to an additional room or suite, providing endless possibilities
for friends and families to create their own Parisian apartment.

Executive Room:

A favourite with many thanks to its generous size and lavish
details Relax back into luxurious chairs and enjoy the glow of natural
light flooding through your tall windows.

Junior Suite:

Lally & Berger. This is your own private retreat
in the very heart of Paris.

Deluxe Junior Suite:
